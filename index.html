// WHO I AM

About.

I am an ICT Security Specialist with deep expertise in penetration testing, secure system design, cloud security, and applied cryptography. At OneTeam Services Group, I collaborate with a dedicated engineering team to secure public transport infrastructure, delivering solutions built on DESFire EV3, SAM-based authentication, and HSM-backed key management workflows including RKI support.

As part of the Defence Trailblazer program at the University of Adelaide's Defence & Security Institute, I work alongside researchers and engineers to advance cybersecurity for autonomous vehicles, drones, and mobile OT systems. This includes co-designing simulated attack and defence environments and developing custom Cisco IOx security applications for mission-critical networks.

Beyond my core roles, I have delivered an invited lecture on memory attacks, volunteered with NATO CCDCOE and BSides Adelaide, and supported offensive and defensive security training across technical and non-technical teams. I am committed to building collaborative, security-first cultures and contributing to South Australia's critical infrastructure protection.
